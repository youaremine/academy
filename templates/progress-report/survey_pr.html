1 INTRODUCTION

1.1 Background

1.1.1 Ozzo Technology (HK) Limited (Ozzo) was commissioned by Transport Department (TD) to carry out the Consultancy Service to conduct Surveys on Public Transport Services in the New Territories (2011-2012) (hereinafter referred to as "the Survey"). The purpose of the Survey is to collect the operational information to form basis for monitoring and planning of public transport services and non-franchised bus services in the New Territories commencement date of the Survey was on {surveyStartDate}.

1.1.2 This progress report is prepared in accordance with Clause 5.5 of the Brief. It outlines the progress that has been made by the Consultants during the period from between {startIncludeDate} to {endIncludeDate}.
